The Business

We are brand partners, not distributors. That distinction defines everything we do.

Vijay Enterprises Global Pvt Ltd manages an exclusive portfolio of India's most recognised consumer brands and leading MNC labels. Complete distribution channel management, market access, retail placement, and supply with full accountability.

Founded by Vijay MN Shetty as part of the Vijay MN Shetty Group, we operate from Bengaluru with a clear purpose: enduring brand relationships, not transactional engagements. Our principle: treat every brand as if it were our own.

Rigorous standards in market conduct, honest performance reporting, consistent shelf presence, and long-term thinking built from 18 years managing brands that every household recognises, now reaching worldwide markets through our global network.
